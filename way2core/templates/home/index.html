About Us:

Way2core Technologies established by PhD scholars and working professionals to fulfill the gap between industrial requirements and academics by providing trainings, workshops and internships on all latest technologies like Wireless communications, Image Processing, Embedded Systems, IoT, Robotics, PCB Designing and Mechatronics with real time projects.

Our Mission:

  • We started to provide workshops, projects and Internships for Electronics and Communication, Electrical, Computer Science and Mechanical students.
  • To support in publishing technical papers in Journals and conferences
  • To provide complete technical support for educational institutes and engineering students in their academic projects
  • Provide complete customer satisfaction by completing the modules within a stipulated time with a best support.
  • To provide real time experience for students on latest technologies in different domains and various tools like IDEs, Software etc.

Our Vision:

  • The Main vision of Way2Core technologies is to be a pioneer in introducing versatile and resourceful professionals to the growing Industries.
  • To accommodate engineering students with a wide spectrum of technologies and domains
  • To be a pathfinder for young talents by providing real time technical knowledge.
  • Become a trademark in conducting complete technical workshops, Training and internships to colleges and universities.
